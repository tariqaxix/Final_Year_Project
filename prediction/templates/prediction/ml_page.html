Machine Learning in Disaster Prediction

Introduction

Machine learning plays a vital role in predicting disaster risks by analyzing weather patterns and environmental data. In this project, we use machine learning models to predict the likelihood and severity of disasters such as droughts, avalanches, and landslides.
